Dr. Norman Borlaug

The man who saved over a billion lives.

Life and Achievements

Norman Borlaug was an American agronomist, humanitarian, and Nobel laureate who has been called "the father of the Green Revolution". He is credited with saving over a billion people worldwide from starvation by developing high-yield, disease-resistant wheat varieties.

"You can't build a peaceful world on empty stomachs and human misery." - Dr. Norman Borlaug
